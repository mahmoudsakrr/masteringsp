FAQ

Q: How will we apply on what has been explained during the sessions?

A: We will work hard on providing problem sets and online sheets for you to solve after each session.

Q: Are the sessions and resources provided enough?

A: This depends on how far you want to go with learning C++. The sessions will help you master using C++ but there’s always room for more and it’s never enough. Learning more about a programming language always helps you grasp the concepts of how everything works behind the scenes.

Q: Will you record the sessions?

A: The sessions will be recorded and uploaded on this GDrive Folder.

Q: How frequent and how long will the sessions be?

A: We’re hoping to have a session every couple of days, and each will be around 2 hours long.

Q: Do I still have a chance to catch up given I know nothing about C++?

A: You’re in the right place! This course (Structured Programming) aims to take you from zero to hero, you aren’t supposed to have any previous C++ knowledge.

Q: Do I have to own a laptop to benefit from the sessions?

A: Not at all! During our introductory session we’ll provide you with multiple alternatives that will provide a near experience to that you’ll get on a laptop.

Q: How much does this journey cost?

A: Nothing! It’s 100% free. Make sure that we will never ever charge you a penny for any of the services that we provide.
